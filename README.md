# ZM405_CattleShrink

To ZERO the scale - Press and Hold the Zero key for 2 seconds and it will zero the scale.
-------------------

To Weigh drafts of cattle:
--------------------------

When the cattle are on the scale and motion is steady, press the 'F1' Key

You will be asked "# Head?"

Enter the number of cattle on the scale and press the 'ZERO' key for ENTER

You will next be asked for Shrink.

Enter the percentage as a whole number  10 for 10%  15 for 15% etc

If there is no shrink simply hit enter.

A line will print out on your printer that looks like this.


Draft Shrink  Head   Draft    Total    Total
              Count  Weight   Head     Weight
----- ------ ------ -------- -------- --------
    1     0     5      2500      5        2500

Now open the gate and remove the cattle from the scale.
Bring on the next group of cattle to the scale and close the gate.

Repeat the steps above for each draft.

** When finished **
After you have finished the last draft press the 'STOP' key in the upper right of the keyboard.

It will then print the final totals of all your drafts.

The printout will look like this:

Draft Shrink  Head   Draft    Total    Total
              Count  Weight   Head     Weight
----- ------ ------ -------- -------- --------
    1     0     5      2500      5        2500
    2     0     5      2500     10        5000
    3     0     5      2500     15        7500
    4     0     5      2500     20       10000
----------------------------------------------
Total Weight =    10000
Total Head =         20
Average Head Wt =   500
